FeedBackLens GENAI

Generative AI Powered Feedback Intelligence Platform

An AI-driven platform designed to transform raw customer feedback into meaningful insights using Large Language Models, Sentiment Analysis and Generative AI.

Project Overview

FeedBackLens GENAI is an intelligent feedback analysis platform that leverages Generative AI to process customer reviews, survey responses and feedback data. The system automatically identifies sentiments, extracts important themes, generates summaries and provides actionable business insights. This enables organizations to understand customer opinions quickly without manually reviewing thousands of feedback records.

Problem Statement

Organizations collect large amounts of customer feedback every day through surveys, reviews and support channels. Analyzing this data manually is time-consuming, inconsistent and inefficient. The objective was to build an AI-powered platform capable of automatically understanding customer sentiment, identifying recurring issues and generating business insights.

Objectives

  • Automate customer feedback analysis.
  • Perform sentiment classification.
  • Generate concise feedback summaries.
  • Identify recurring themes and concerns.
  • Provide actionable business insights.
  • Visualize feedback trends through dashboards.
  • Reduce manual analysis effort.
