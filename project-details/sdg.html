SENSE SDG Awareness Platform

Full Stack Web Application

An educational platform designed to promote awareness of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through interactive learning modules, quizzes, videos and engagement activities.

Project Overview

The SENSE SDG Awareness Platform was developed to educate students about the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) defined by the United Nations. The platform provides interactive learning experiences, educational resources, quizzes, videos and engagement tools to increase awareness about sustainability and global development.

Problem Statement

Many students are unaware of the Sustainable Development Goals and their importance in creating a better future. Traditional awareness programs often lack engagement and interactive learning opportunities. The goal was to build a digital platform that makes learning about SDGs more engaging, accessible and measurable.

Objectives

  • Promote awareness of all 17 SDGs.
  • Provide interactive educational content.
  • Track student learning progress.
  • Conduct quizzes to assess understanding.
  • Encourage participation through engagement features.
  • Create a centralized sustainability learning platform.
